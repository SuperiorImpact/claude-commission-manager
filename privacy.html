Privacy Policy

Claude Commission Manager — Superior Impact, Inc. · Effective April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Superior Impact, Inc. (“we,” “us”) handles information in connection with the Claude Commission Manager software (“Software”).

1. Who This Covers

The Software is a private, single-tenant integration used internally by Superior Impact, Inc. It is not offered to the public and has no external end users beyond authorized Superior Impact personnel.

2. Information We Access

When authorized by the QuickBooks Online (“QBO”) account owner via Intuit’s OAuth flow, the Software accesses QBO data required to perform its bookkeeping functions, including: company information, customers and vendors, invoices, items, payments, and general-ledger transactions. We do not access QBO payroll data or bank login credentials.

3. How We Use the Information

Accessed data is used solely to (a) create and list invoices, (b) record and apply payments, (c) generate commission and reconciliation reports, and (d) maintain a local audit record of bookkeeping activity. Data is not used for marketing, advertising, or resale.

4. How We Store the Information

The Software runs on computing infrastructure controlled by Superior Impact, Inc. OAuth tokens and QBO responses are stored only as needed to perform authorized operations. We do not maintain a long-term copy of QBO transactional data outside of the reports and audit records generated for Superior Impact’s internal use.

5. Sharing

We do not sell or share QBO data with third parties. Data may be transmitted only between (a) Intuit’s QBO APIs, (b) Superior Impact’s own internal systems, and (c) authorized service providers (such as the operating system and network infrastructure) strictly as necessary to operate the Software.

6. Security

We use Intuit’s OAuth 2.0 protocol for authentication, transmit all QBO API traffic over TLS, and restrict access to the Software to authorized Superior Impact personnel. No system is perfectly secure; we make reasonable commercial efforts to protect the data the Software handles.

7. Retention & Deletion

You may disconnect the Software from QBO at any time via the “Apps” section of your QBO account. Upon disconnection, the Software stops accessing QBO data and any cached OAuth tokens are invalidated. Local audit records generated before disconnection are retained for Superior Impact’s internal bookkeeping compliance.

8. Your Rights

Because the Software is used only by authorized Superior Impact personnel acting on Superior Impact’s behalf, this Policy does not create consumer-facing rights. Intuit’s own privacy policy governs the handling of QBO data inside QuickBooks.

9. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
